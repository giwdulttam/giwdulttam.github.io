Research

Mathematics.

publications ⋅ presentations ⋅ events ⋅ people

I work at the intersection of probability theory and functional analysis. My current main area of research is free probability and bi-free probability, which generalize classical probability theory. I am currently working to extend several results and constructions from free probability theory to bi-free probability theory. I am also interested in stochastic analysis and the Malliavin-Stein method and its applications in random matrix theory.
