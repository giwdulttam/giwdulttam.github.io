Research

Mathematics.

My research focuses on stochastic analysis and quantitative central limit theorems. I am particularly interested in interacting particle systems and the random fluctuation fields that arise from many-particle dynamics. I use tools from Malliavin calculus and the Malliavin-Stein method to seek explicit rates of convergence of these fluctuations to Gaussian limits. I am also broadly interested in random matrix theory, free probability theory and stochastic partial differential equations, as well as using mathematical models to understand music.
